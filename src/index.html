Do you want to join our mission to reshape the future of public services?

We believe in the power of 21st-century organisations to deliver the highest quality, citizen-centred services that have a lasting impact for all. We’re looking for a Service Design Intern with an enquiring mind who is passionate about public sector change, people and all things design!

Progress in your career as a service designer by:

  • spending an initial 9 months in an industry-leading organisation
  • having a supportive design team around you and a dedicated mentor who will help you develop
  • designing services by bringing end-users, project stakeholders and clients on the journey with you
  • working with a range of clients from the public and health sector
  • learning to navigate the complexity of public services in the UK and how to build and hold a vision while delivering change
  • developing new approaches and tools for design research, ideation and delivery of new services

We’re looking for a current or recent graduate:

  • from design, service design, strategic design, visual design or similar field
  • with knowledge about service design, methods and tools such as as-is mapping, scenarios, to be journey, blueprinting and prototyping
  • who is detail oriented and passionate about public service design

You’ll work in an environment that:

  • is based out of our studio in the fantastic London Bridge area
  • involves traveling around the UK to client sites
  • brings the team together every month with events such as escape rooms, go karting and mini golf
  • is an inspiring, social, collaborative and playful work environment, with an open-minded and diverse culture
  • provides cross-office inspiration trips and local inspiration sessions year-round
